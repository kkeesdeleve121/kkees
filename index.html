We care about your educational journey – this is our promise to you

At HHL, we believe that learning is a journey that should never end. Times are very uncertain currently and we are here to help you with any concern you may have regarding your own personal educational journey.

That is why we have created the HHL Promise. It is a promise that allows you to start your graduate program at HHL to secure the best possible future for yourself.

The HHL Promise

Our MBA Promise

We are here to help you with any concern you may have regarding your own personal educational journey. That is our MBA Promise. It allows you to start your career in Germany and beyond.

We will enroll you in fall 2020, so you can experience the full HHL spirit starting from day one. We will begin with classes on campus in January 2021 because we know that your  MBA experience depends on networking and interactive exchange.

We care about your education – this is our promise to you. 

Our MBA Promise

Our Master in Management Promise

We want to make sure to find the right solutions for your personal educational journey without compromising on the excellence of our programs. That is why we have created our Master in Management Promise. It is a promise that allows you to start your graduate program at HHL this fall.

We will start the new classes as planned in September 2020.  We intend to open the campus and have lectures at our three locations. However, we are prepared to begin with online courses in the first few weeks, if overall safety and hygiene regulations require us to do so.

We care about your education – this is our promise to you.
